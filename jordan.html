The Jordan Options

Did you know that the Jordan brand was named after Michael Jordan? He is a former American basketball player who led the Chicago Bulls to six NBA championships and won the Most Valuable Player Award five times. In 1985, Nike gave Jordan and his signature line of sneakers and apparel a unique logo - clearly, this line was created to be very different from Nike's previous basketball efforts. Designer Peter Moore was given the task of coming up with the first Air Jordan shoe. The Air Jordan 1 featured the Nike Swoosh on the mid panel and a newly designed wings logo on the upper ankle.
