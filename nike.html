The Nike Options

Nike started in January 25, 1964, but was first under the company name Blue Ribbon Sports located in Beaverton, Oregon, by Bill Bowerman, a track-and-field coach at the University of Oregon, and his former student Phil Knight. They launched the Nike brand shoe in 1972 and renamed their compnay to Nike, Inc., in 1978. By the early 21st century, the "swoosh" logo was recognized throughout the world, partly due to their strategic use of endoresements from athletes such as Michael Jordan, Mia Hamm, Roger Federer, and Tiger Woods.
