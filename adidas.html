The Adidas Options

Adidas started in August 18, 1949 when Adi Dassler, at the age of 49, created a company with a mere 47 employees in the small town of Herzogenaurach, Germany. On the same day, he registered a shoe that included the registration of the soon-to-become-famous Adidas 3-Stripes. After a few successful years in the shoe business, the compnay expanded into apparel in 1967 when the Franz Beckenbauer tracksuit model celebrated its debut.It became the first piece of apparel for Adidas and opened a whole new business to a company that, so far, was only famous for shoes.
